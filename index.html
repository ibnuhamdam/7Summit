7. Gunung Bukit Raya

Katingan, Kalimantan Tengah.
2.278 mdpl
Gunung Bukit Raya is one of the seven highest peaks in Indonesia and is located in West Kalimantan. Heading to the top does not only require extra energy, but also costs a lot. Remembering the place is in the interior of the forest.

6. Gunung Binaiya

Pulau Seram, Maluku.
3.005 mdpl
Mount Binaiya is the highest point on the Indonesian island of Seram. At an elevation of 3,027 metres, it is one of the one hundred most topographically prominent peaks on Earth

5. Gunung Latimojong

Kabupaten Enrekang, Sulawesi
3.378 mdpl
Latimojong, Rantemario is a mountain located in the province of South Sulawesi, Sulawesi, Indonesia. At 3,478 metres, it is the highest mountain on the island, although some sources state Mount Rantekombola as the highest point. Locally, Rantemario is more commonly referred to as Latimojong
